Caffeine: Good for your health !

Caffeine, a xanthine alkaloid compund, is consumed widely and daily by humans, as it present in several regular beverages such as tea, coffe, soda beverages, and some drugs. It's consumption riggers arousal and alertness, improve mood, and the release of catecholamines, which induce beneficial effects of human behavior.

Nonetheless, caffeine has been related to other beneficial effects such as antioxidant and anti-inflamantory actions that are extremely important to human health, altering the cellular redox and inflammatory status in a dose dependent-manner.

The ergogenic effects of caffeine might be due to several factors, such as enhanced substrate utilization, fatigue delay, and staying awake.

In sports, caffeine appears to improve physical performance in both trained and untrained individuals. Consumpt caffeine can enhance various aspects of exercise performance in many sports, but are not limited to muscular endurance, movement velocity, and muscular strength, sprinting, jumping, and throwing performance.
